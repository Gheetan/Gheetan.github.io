Medal of Heroes

Awarded for exceptional bravery and service to the Empire.

Eligibility: Available to both citizens and non-citizens who have demonstrated outstanding courage.

Medal of Gheetan

Recognizes individuals who have made significant contributions to the advancement and prosperity of the Empire.

Eligibility: Awarded to citizens who have shown dedication and service in various fields.

Order of the Brown Bear

Honors long-term service and commitment to the Imperial government and its institutions.

Eligibility: Granted to citizens who have served the Empire for extended periods with distinction.

Medal of The United Canadian Micronations

Awarded to thowes who have served in the UCM

Eligibility: leders of the UCM or have done a great serves to the UCM.

Medal of Arcadia

Awarded for outstanding contributions to the unity, prosperity, and strength of Arcadia within the Great Gheetan Empire.

Eligibility:

  • Citizens of Arcadia who have significantly advanced the growth and cultural development of the realm.
  • Non-citizens who have supported Arcadia through diplomatic or military efforts.
  • Individuals who have strengthened the bond between Arcadia and the Empire in any meaningful way.
  • even though the medal is called the Medal of Arcadia it may only be given or revoced by the Emperor of Gheetan
