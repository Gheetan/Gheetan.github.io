Medal of the Authority

Awarded for:

  • Honors the founding and sovereign leadership of a recognized micronation.
  • Bestowed upon those whose acts of nation-creation demonstrate legitimacy, structure, stability, and a clear sense of law and identity—principles that mirror the founding of the Great Gheetan Empire itself.

    • Eligibility:

  • Awarded solely to micronational heads of state, emperors, kings, or founders who hold sovereign authority.
  • Only those whose nation has reached a threshold of structure, population, or diplomatic presence, as judged by the Emperor, may qualify.
  • This medal cannot be self-nominated and must be conferred personally by the Emperor of Gheetan.

    • Copyright: This model is officially logged in the Gheetan Copyright Log

    order of the Emperor

    Awarded for:

  • The highest civilian or military decoration of the Great Gheetan Empire.
  • This order is granted exclusively to individuals who have shown exceptional loyalty, service, or contribution to the Emperor personally or to the stability, expansion, and prosperity of the Gheetan Empire as a whole.
  • Recipients of this order are forever recorded in the Imperial Annals as having served the Throne itself.

    • Eligibility:

  • Only those who have proven themselves beyond duty—through unwavering devotion, exceptional achievements in support of the Empire, or lifetime service to the Emperor—are eligible.
  • This honor may be awarded to citizens, foreigners, heads of state, or high-ranking imperial officials.
  • The Order of the Emperor may not be applied for, requested, or expected—it is granted solely at the will and discernment of Your Imperial Majesty.
