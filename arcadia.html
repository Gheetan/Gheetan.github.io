Introduction

The Kingdom Realm of Arcadia is a sovereign monarchical territory and loyal realm of the Great Gheetan Empire, governed with honor and devotion by His Lordship, the Lord High King of Arcadia. Arcadia is bound eternally to the Gheetan Throne by the sacred Treaty of Eternal Unity, preserving noble customs while upholding Gheetan supremacy.

Monarchical Hierarchy

Supreme Monarch: His Imperial Majesty, the Emperor of Gheeta — Sovereign Ruler of All Realms
Realm Monarch: His Lordship, the Lord High King of Arcadia — Monarch of the Realm, under Imperial Rule

Powers of His Lordship

  • Internal Governance: Manages Arcadia's domestic affairs, laws, and appointments
  • Noble Council: Oversees Arcadian council and internal noble titles
  • Local Justice: Controls courts and civil judgment within the realm
  • Cultural Authority: Directs cultural identity, ceremonies, and symbols
  • Limited Diplomacy: Permitted symbolic and cultural diplomacy only
  • Honor Guard: May raise ceremonial military force with imperial approval

Powers of His Imperial Majesty

  • Supreme Sovereignty: Arcadia is under the absolute authority of Gheeta
  • Foreign Affairs: Sole control over diplomacy and treaties
  • Military Deployment: Full authority over armed forces beyond the realm
  • Succession Oversight: Arcadian throne succession requires imperial confirmation
  • Lawmaking Supremacy: Imperial law overrides all realm law
  • Title Confirmation: Grants or revokes all noble titles in Arcadia
  • Dispute Arbitration: Settles disputes through the Imperial Court
  • Unity Enforcement: May intervene in Arcadian affairs to ensure imperial loyalty

Heraldry and Unity

“Honor Through Loyalty, Glory Through Unity”
