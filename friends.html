Citizenship & Immigration Policy

Who Can Become a Citizen?

Citizenship in the Great Gheetan Empire is granted **only by the Emperor**. Individuals cannot apply; they must be offered citizenship directly.

Are There Any Criteria for Offering Citizenship?

No. The Emperor may grant citizenship to any individual at his discretion.

Can Citizenship Be Revoked?

Yes. The Emperor has the authority to revoke citizenship at any time for any reason.

Do Citizens Have Any Responsibilities?

No. There are no mandatory duties or responsibilities for citizens.

Does the Empire Allow Dual Citizenship?

Yes. Citizens of the Great Gheetan Empire may hold citizenship in other nations or micronations without restriction.

Government Finances & Defense

Currency & Economy

Official Currency: The **Glawar**

Economic Structure: The economy is fully controlled by the Emperor. There are currently no major industries or sources of wealth.

Trade & Commerce: The Great Gheetan Empire does not currently engage in trade with other nations or micronations.

Taxation & Budget

Taxes: The Great Gheetan Empire does not impose taxes on its citizens.

Budget Management: The Emperor controls the allocation of the national budget.

Spending Priorities: Budget funds are allocated to the Emperor, and people may decide what it goes toward.

Military & Defense

Military Name: the imperial guard.

Active Members: There are no active members at this time.

Main Purpose: The military exists for **defense and alliances**.

Mandatory Service: Military service is **not required** for citizens.

Alliances & Defense Agreements: The Great Gheetan Empire has military alliances and defense agreements with other nations.
